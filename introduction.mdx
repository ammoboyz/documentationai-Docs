## Overview

Tyler Durden provides a centralized space for your project documentation. You create, organize, and collaborate on docs using familiar MDX syntax. Manage multiple projects with versioned spaces, search across content, and integrate with your workflows seamlessly.

This default space helps you get started quickly. Scale from single-page guides to comprehensive API references without complexity.

## Quick Start

Get your first documentation space live in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Account" icon="user-plus">
    Sign up at `https://tylerdurden.com/register` with your email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="New Space" icon="plus">
    Click **New Space** and name it for your project, like "MyApp Docs".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Document" icon="file-text">
    Create `introduction.mdx` and paste your content.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish" icon="rocket">
    Hit **Publish** to generate your site at `https://docs.tylerdurden.com/myapp`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<CodeGroup tabs="MDX Example">
  ```mdx
  ## Hello World

  <Callout kind="success">
    Your first doc is live!
  </Callout>
  ```
</CodeGroup>

## Why Choose Tyler Durden

You select Tyler Durden for its balance of power and simplicity. Unlike static site generators requiring builds, Tyler Durden offers live editing and instant previews. Teams prefer it over wikis for MDX flexibility and Git workflows.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Start with the default space. Upgrade for teams and custom themes using brand color `#3B82F6`.
</Callout>
