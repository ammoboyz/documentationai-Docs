## Overview

Sign up using your email, then create your first project space.

## Steps

1. Go to `https://tylerdurden.com` and click "Sign Up"
2. Verify your email
3. Log in and choose "New Project"
4. Name your project and add your first document

## Need help?
Contact support anytime: `support@tylerdurden.com`
