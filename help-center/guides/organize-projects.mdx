## Overview

Organize all documentation in project spaces.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a project">
    ````
    Click "New Project" in your dashboard.
    Name your project. Example: `My Portfolio Docs`
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add documents and folders">
    ````
    Use "Add Document" or "Add Folder" to structure your docs.
    Drag and drop to reorder.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite collaborators">
    ````
    Go to Project Settings > "Invite User"
    Enter email addresses, set permissions.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
