---
title: Cannot Access Dashboard
description: Fix dashboard access problems in Tyler Durden.
---

## Symptoms

- Stuck on loading screen
- Error: "Dashboard not available"

## Causes

- Internet connection problem
- Outdated browser

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Refresh and check connection">
    ````
    Reload the page and ensure you are online.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    ````
    Try in incognito mode or clear cookies/cache.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update browser">
    ````
    Use latest Chrome, Firefox, or Edge for best results.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
